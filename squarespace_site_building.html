Case Study: Personal Site Building in Squarespace

Evidence type: Personal hands-on experience
Platform: Squarespace

Experience

I have used Squarespace extensively for personal site-building work. This involved more than selecting a template: I worked with page structure, navigation, content hierarchy, forms, visual consistency, mobile presentation, and the practical relationship between a site's purpose and the actions a visitor needs to take.

Transferable knowledge

  • information architecture and page hierarchy;
  • translating a non-technical goal into site structure;
  • domain and publishing concepts;
  • forms and contact flows;
  • responsive presentation;
  • debugging layout or configuration problems;
  • explaining site changes in plain language.

Limitation

Squarespace experience is not presented as WordPress expertise. The WordPress/WooCommerce work in this portfolio is separately labelled as a technical lab. The value of the Squarespace experience is that I already understand site-building decisions and customer goals, while I am deliberately building platform-specific WordPress knowledge.
