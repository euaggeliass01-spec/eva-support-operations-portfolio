# Remote Hospitality Operations and Guest Support

**Evidence type:** Real professional experience  
**Duration:** Four years  
**Scale:** Two short-term rental properties

## Operating metrics

- Current host profile: 4.78/5 average across 9 published reviews.
- Approximately 15 reservations per month.
- Approximately 40 guest conversations per week.
- Six cleaners, maintenance providers, owners, and local collaborators coordinated.

## Process improvements

- Automated message flows for pre-arrival, check-in, Wi-Fi, property use, and checkout.
- Remote troubleshooting routines for Wi-Fi, television, access, and check-in.
- Clear step-by-step instructions for guests with different levels of technical confidence.
- Escalation to on-site collaborators only when physical intervention was necessary.

## Published feedback

Reviews specifically mention fast and precise communication, easy check-in, clear instructions, preparation, cleanliness, and responsiveness.
